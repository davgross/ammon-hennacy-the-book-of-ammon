Colophon

The Book of Ammon
was first published in 1965 by
Ammon Hennacy. This ebook is based on the expanded fifth printing, from 1970.

This ebook was produced by
David M. Gross,
and is based on a hardcopy of that fifth printing, along with digital scans of an earlier version of Hennacy’s autobiography (The Autobiography of a Catholic Anarchist) which were scanned in 2007 by
Scott L. Williams and which are available at the
Internet Archive.

The cover page is adapted from
Jean-François Millet’s Man with a Hoe
The cover and title pages feature the
League Spartan and Sorts Mill Goudy
typefaces created in 2014 and 2009 by
The League of Moveable Type.

This is the first edition of this ebook.
This edition was released on
September 5, 2016, 6:56 p.m.
