Imprint

This ebook is based on a physical copy of the fifth printing of the book, and on digital scan and partial transcription of an earlier Hennacy autobiography produced for Archive.org, and on its transcription found at Wikisource.

The book was published in 1970 without a copyright notice, and so it is in the public domain. This accords with the anarchist principles of the author, who would not have asked the government to help him defend a copyright in his writings.

The author wishes to express thanks for the use of quotations from Karl Jung, and from the poets Robert Frost, Lillian Spencer, and Vachel Lindsay.
